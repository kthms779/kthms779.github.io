2019

December:

Smart solar pumps use big data to stop Africa being sucked dry (18/12/19):
This system that uses real-time big data collected from pump sensors calculates groundwater extraction rates and levels. Data collected includes energy usage and pump speed at each location. Then, adjustments can be made to the system to better suit irrigation needs and technical performance, improving efficiency. Improving this water system will improve agriculture in Africa, and thus better ensure food security for the people, which should contribute to solving the global food crisis.

2020

June:

Sundar Pichai brings in big data policy change in Google; users’ location data to be erased after 18 months (25/06/20):
There has always been a large controversy with how tech companies such as Google, Amazon, Facebook, etc. store excessive amounts of user data and utilize that to manipulate users by customizing ads and posts to appeal to them, as well as worries of its vulnerability. Google has decided to bring about a policy change in order to fight against said controversy - the maximum period of user location data that is stored in Google’s database is now 18 months before it gets automatically deleted. Google has also made it easier for mobile users to switch to incognito mode on their browser, which was infamously difficult before. With these changes, I believe that Google is moving in the right direction to improve customer engagement and satisfaction by protecting the integrity of user data.

September:

Britain plans hiring spree to harness big data in Covid-19 pandemic recovery (9/09/20):
Britain’s Prime Minister Boris Johnson has called for the training of hundreds of data scientists as part of ‘The National Data’ project to drive an economic recovery after the COVID-19 pandemic through the better and smarter use of data and technology in policy-making. Digital Minister Oliver Dowden notes that if the country shares high-quality data quickly, efficiently and ethically, it will be able to recover much better. This is certainly the case right now when passing the data of the number of infections in a particular location to local authorities is crucial to stop outbreaks.
