Global Internet of Things (IoT) Solutions & Market Opportunities to 2024 (9/12/19):
A 16-page research report by BusinessWire has predicted global IoT solutions and market opportunities in the next 5 years (till late 2024). Honestly, the research isn’t that surprising considering the obvious course that technological development has taken in the last decade. For me, the most interesting point in the report is how “5G and IoT enabled smart machines represent a $1.2B global opportunity by 2024”. I currently use a 4G mobile phone, and have a smart fridge in my house, which is incredible in and of itself. It’s crazy to think that the technology can be developed even further, and I can’t wait to see what the tech industry has to offer.

What is Artificial Intelligence of Things? What is AIoT (20/12/19):
With the combination of AI (Artificial Intelligence) and IoT (Internet of Things), I think humanity will evolve much more rapidly. Incorporating AI into everyday tasks such as in driving vehicles will increase the efficiency of the device, in my opinion. For instance, taking the vehicle example the article mentioned, we are able to easily collect data in vehicles from sensors, radars and the GPS, which can be uploaded onto the internet, allowing for the AI to monitor vehicle maintenance, driver behaviour, and more. AIoT has already been implemented in Tesla’s autopilot systems.
