Japanese Researchers Teaching Robots to Repair Themselves (5/12/19):
A PR2 robot has been taught to repair itself by researchers from the University of Tokyo, though there are some limitations. This is a cool discovery in the world of robots! Robots being able to repair themselves may not seem like a big deal, but it makes the system much more efficient. As stated in the article, they become more independent and capable. We are now one step closer for robot world domination!

You’ve Heard Of Robots; What Are Cobots? (15/12/19):
“Cobots” stands for collaborative robots- robots who work in conjunction with humans. Unlike regular robots, they have been built to work together with humans, so they aid them in feats that are usually not able to be done by regular people. This includes providing super strength, precision, data capabilities, and more. These cobots can be used in the manufacturing industry, warehouses, etc.
